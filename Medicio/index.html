    Cardiology

    Comprehensive heart care delivered by specialists using the latest diagnostic and treatment technology.

    Our Cardiology Department is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart-related conditions such as hypertension, coronary artery disease, heart failure, and arrhythmias. With advanced cardiac imaging, interventional procedures, and a compassionate care team, we ensure each patient receives personalized attention for better heart health and long-term recovery.

    Neurology

    Expert care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders — focused on restoring function and improving quality of life.

    Our Neurology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating a wide range of neurological conditions, including stroke, epilepsy, migraines, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson’s disease, and more. With a multidisciplinary team and cutting-edge technology, we provide accurate assessments, personalized treatment plans, and ongoing support for patients and their families.

    Hepatology

    Specialized liver care backed by advanced diagnostics and compassionate treatment approaches.

    Our Hepatology Department focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of liver, gallbladder, biliary tree, and pancreatic disorders. From hepatitis and fatty liver disease to cirrhosis and liver cancer, our team delivers expert care using modern imaging, lab analysis, and personalized medical therapies — all within a patient-centered environment.

    Pediatrics

    Comprehensive care for infants, children, and adolescents in a safe and nurturing environment.

    Our Pediatrics Department is dedicated to the health and development of young patients — from newborns to teenagers. We provide preventive care, immunizations, growth monitoring, and treatment for a wide range of childhood illnesses. Our compassionate team works closely with families to ensure every child receives personalized, age-appropriate care that supports both physical and emotional well-being.

    Ophthalmologists

    Expert care for your vision — from routine eye exams to advanced surgical procedures.

    Our Ophthalmology Department offers comprehensive eye care services including diagnostics, treatment of eye diseases, and corrective surgeries. Whether you're managing glaucoma, cataracts, retinal conditions, or simply need a vision checkup, our specialists use cutting-edge technology to preserve and enhance your sight. We’re committed to helping you see life more clearly — safely and effectively.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to some of the most common questions about our services, appointments, and processes.

    How can I schedule an appointment?

    You can book an appointment directly through our website using the appointment form or by calling our customer care line. Our team will assist you with selecting a date, time, and preferred department.

    Do I need a referral to see a specialist?

    No, a referral is not required to consult with our specialists. However, if you were referred by another doctor, please bring your medical records for a more accurate diagnosis and treatment plan.

    What insurance plans do you accept?

    We accept a wide range of insurance providers. For a complete list, please visit our insurance page or contact our billing department. We also offer self-pay options for uninsured patients.

    What should I bring to my first visit?

    Kindly bring a valid ID, any previous medical records, a list of current medications, and your insurance card (if applicable). Arriving 15 minutes early is also recommended for registration.

    Are telehealth consultations available?

    Yes, we offer secure telehealth consultations for non-emergency cases. You can book a virtual appointment through our portal and consult with our doctors from the comfort of your home.

    How can I access my test results?

    Test results can be accessed through our patient portal. You will receive an email or SMS notification once your results are ready. For urgent results, a member of our team will contact you directly.
